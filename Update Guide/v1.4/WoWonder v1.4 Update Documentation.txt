1) How To Update

- BACKUP, BACKUP, AND BACKUP everything, files, DATABASE.

- Upload "update.php" file located on "Update Guide/v1.4" folder to your server.

- Upload and overwrite ALL files/folders (SELECT ALL) file(s) located inside "Update Guide/v1.4/Script" folder to your server, if you have renamed your theme, please open "Update Guide/v1.4/Script/themes/wowonder/" and upload & overwrite ALL files/folders to your theme folder.

- Open http://www.YOURSITE.COM/update.php

- After finishing the update, make sure you delete the "update.php" file.

- Refresh your page, Done !
