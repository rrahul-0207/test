1) How To Update

- BACKUP, BACKUP, BACKUP, AND BACKUP everything, files, DATABASE. {THIS IS IMPORTANT UPDATE AND WILL EFFECT YOUR DB}.

- Upload and overwrite ALL files/folders (SELECT ALL) file(s) located inside "Update Guide/v1.4.3/Script" folder to your server, if you have renamed your theme, please open "Update Guide/v1.4.3/Script/themes/wowonder/" and upload & overwrite ALL files/folders to your theme folder.

- Upload "update.php" file located on "Update Guide/v1.4.3" folder to your server.

- Once the files are uploaded, open and run http://www.YOURSITE.COM/update.php

- Note: Don't run "update.php" file till all the files are uploaded.

- Note: The "update.php" file runtime process may take up to 5 min, please don't close it till the site is updated.

- Refresh your page, clear your browser cache, Done !

- For more information, please read our FAQ http://docs.wowonder.com/#faq
