1) How To Update

- BACKUP, BACKUP, AND BACKUP everything, files, DATABASE.

- Upload and overwrite ALL files/folders (SELECT ALL) file(s) located inside "Update Guide/v1.4.4.1/Script" folder to your server, if you have renamed your theme, please open "Update Guide/v1.4.4.1/Script/themes/wowonder/" and upload & overwrite ALL files/folders to your theme folder.

- Refresh your page, Done !
